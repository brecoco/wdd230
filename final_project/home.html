About Carlsbad

"With mild, year-round temperatures, Carlsbad draws in visitors seeking rugged adventures as well as relaxing family fun. South Carlsbad State Beach offers visitors a remote camping adventure, situated among rugged cliffs, but allowing room for surfing, swimming, fishing and skin diving. Meanwhile, Tamarack Beach provides access for joggers, bikers and walkers along the Carlsbad Seawall and Frazee Beach offers a low-key locale for swimmers in search of calmer waters. Take a break from the sand and water at nearby LEGOLAND Amusement Park, with its hands-on attractions, rides and shows."
