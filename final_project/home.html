About the Carlsbad City

About Carlsbad

"With mild, year-round temperatures, Carlsbad draws in visitors seeking rugged adventures as well as relaxing family fun. South Carlsbad State Beach offers visitors a remote camping adventure, situated among rugged cliffs, but allowing room for surfing, swimming, fishing and skin diving. Meanwhile, Tamarack Beach provides access for joggers, bikers and walkers along the Carlsbad Seawall and Frazee Beach offers a low-key locale for swimmers in search of calmer waters."

What to do in Carlsbad

"Also known as the Village by the Sea, Carlsbad is one of the most picturesque locations in California. Though it is located between major cities like Los Angeles and San Diego, Carlsbad is a tiny place filled with scenic beauty and is reminscent of Europe. With luxurious golf courses, good wine from famous California vineyards, and pristine beaches, Carlsbad is one of the prettiest locations to spend a holiday.Take a break from the sand and water at nearby LEGOLAND Amusement Park, with its hands-on attractions, rides and shows."
