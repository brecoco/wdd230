Target Audience

This site is directed to people who are interested in knowing more about the temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints located in Brazil. It is a site where you can find information such as photos, address, construction date, operating status. The temples of the LDS Church are beautiful constructions that draw attention and arouse curiosity of many people. Brazil in recent years has gained several temples and that's why the importance of building a website with centralized information about Brazilian temples.
